About Factoids

Our Responsibilities and Vision

The Factoids is committed to providing trustful and valuable information about COVID-19. We care about the elderly living in Victoria and hope we can prptect them from being misled by false information.

Frequently ask questions

We are always dedicated to give you the best support and that's why we have made a free site for you.

  • 1. How does Factoid help you?

    Factoid contains almost all information about COVID-19 from the official platform, guarantees the authenticity of the information, and updates the information in real time. Presenting this information in text, pictures, and video to help you avoid being misled by fake COVID-19 news.

  • 2. Why chose Factoid?

    In order to facilitate the use of older people, all content and categories on the website are easy to understand. When users are confused about the authenticity of COVID-19 information or news, visiting our website is your best choice.

  • 3. Will I get support if I have symptoms or I suspect I may have a Coronavirus?

    Of course, we'll do our best to help you. First you should make sure you are in self-quarantine. You can contact us or call 1800 675 398 for support. If you suspect you may have a Coronavirus (COVID-19) call the dedicated hotline -- Open 24 hours,7 days. Please keep Triple Zero (000) for emergencies only.
