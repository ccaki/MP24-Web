About VIC senior Co-Pro

Our Vision

As a fact checking website for Victorian Seniors, the Vic Senior Co-Pro is committed to nurturing and bringing awareness in the lives of Victorian Seniors (aged 60-80 years) by facilitating better wellbeing through solutions that would help them distinguish the ‘facts’ and ‘fiction’ rampant due to Covid-19.

Factoids strives to provide trustful and valuable information about COVID-19 as we care about the community of our seniors residing in Victoria and hope we can safeguard them from being misled by false information.
